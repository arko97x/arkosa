Of Poetry, Play, and Humour —
Designing emotive & relatable experiences
This website is an attempt to make my work available to all those who were not able to engage with my work on the day of the gallery walk (14 May 2022). The site annotates and delves into the various explorations that I undertook over the course of five months. The explorations have spanned both the physical and the digital realms. Since their inception, a lot of these works and ideas have encountered several minds on their way to execution. I shall name these co-designers at appropriate junctures. I wish to unpack projects under the broad theme of storytelling through the agencies of humour, play, and poetry (rhyme). As will be evident, poetry introduces an aspect of playfulness to the mix. There are several questions around art, design, the practice of making, etc. that I try to address throughout this website.

To briefly lay out the timeline, all works, and concepts in this document were created at some point during a 5-month period - January through May of 2022. I present a total of seven pieces of work. Some of them possess a strand of play in their DNA. Some of them explore the power of language, via poetry, to accentuate the playfulness and thus elicit joy. It is interesting to note, though, that poetry was something that I noticed while sifting through my body of work. While I’ve always enjoyed the rhythm associated with rhyme, I hadn’t necessarily thought of its ability to elicit emotions. This discovery ties in well with my intial line of inquiry of ‘The Art of Eliciting Emotions’. There are questions that evolved while annotating the works that reside on this site, which I intend to bring up under the respective works.
